How it plays

  • Curl — close your fingers to darken the tone (low-pass filter closes).
  • Tilt — rotate your wrist to bend the pitch up or down.
  • Spread — fan your fingers to widen the sound (adds a detuned second voice).
  • Height — raise your hand to raise the volume; a closed fist mutes it.
